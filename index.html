।। मौनं सर्वार्थ साधनम् ।।

(Silence is an instrument that achieves all ends.)

।। नास्ति ज्ञानसमं तेजः ।।

(There is no brilliance equal to knowledge.)

।। प्रबलः कर्मसिद्धान्तः ।।

(The law of karma is very powerful)

।। प्रेम्णा बहु धैर्यं गृह्णाति ।।

(Love takes a lot of patience.)

।। मनः एव बृहत्तमः भ्रमः अस्ति ।।

(The mind is the biggest illusion.)
